Subtraction Cover Up

Random Number: 5

How to Play

Use the slider to choose how many circles you want (2-20).

Click on any circle to toggle it on or off. When a circle is "on", it shows a black dot.

Click "Random Number" to fill all circles and get a random number to subtract.

Practice subtraction by covering up (turning off) the random number of circles to see what remains.
